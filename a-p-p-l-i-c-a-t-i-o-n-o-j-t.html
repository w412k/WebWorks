SALARY : N/A

Company: TechStartUpCo 

TechStartUpCo is seeking a web developer intern with a passion for technology and a desire to learn and grow with our team. As a member of TechStartUpCo, you will work closely with our Senior Web Developer. 

Responsibilities: Assist in developing and maintaining websites for our clients based on their specific needs and goals. Collaborate with team members to understand client requirements and tailor web development to meet their needs. Provide feedback and make necessary revisions to web development projects to ensure client satisfaction. Use programming languages and web development tools to deliver high-quality websites. Communicate effectively with clients and team members to ensure timely completion of web development projects.  

Requirements: Must have a strong understanding of web development principles and programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Must have a desire to learn and grow in the field of web development. Must be able to work independently and as part of a team. Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English. Ability to use web development tools such as Visual Studio Code and Git.  

At TechStartUpCo, we are dedicated to providing our clients with innovative web development solutions that exceed their expectations. We are committed to fostering a collaborative and supportive team environment that encourages creativity and growth. If you are passionate about web development and are looking for an exciting opportunity to learn and grow with a dynamic team, we invite you to apply for this internship.
